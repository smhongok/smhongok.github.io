\noindent
My research focuses on the \textbf{inversion of deep generative models}, spanning structural design and optimization-based methods.
I develop principled algorithms for \textbf{exact and approximate inversion} of diffusion models and normalizing flows, with applications to \textbf{image editing}, \textbf{posterior sampling}, and \textbf{inverse problems in imaging}.
My doctoral dissertation on this topic was recognized with the \textbf{Distinguished Ph.D. Dissertation Award}.
